"Thabat" App Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to the Thabat app. Your privacy and your digital and psychological safety are our top priorities. This policy clearly explains how we handle your data in a secure environment based on a "Local-First" architecture.

1. Local Processing Architecture (100% Data Protection)

All physiological biometric data, personal diaries, and mood tracking logs are exclusively saved and processed "locally" within the encrypted storage space on your phone. The Thabat app does not use any external cloud servers to process your emotions, meaning your private data never leaves your phone.

2. Accessibility Service and Focus Shield

"Guardian of Wisdom" relies on Android's AccessibilityService to detect doomscrolling and digital fatigue.

  • Zero-Keylogging Policy: We never read the content of your private messages, we do not record your screen, and we do not transmit any personal content from your apps. The service only translates your interactions into mathematical "fatigue scores" instantaneously and locally.

3. Microphone and Audio Data Processing

The audio sensor in Thabat uses the microphone in the background to detect states of high arousal (such as screaming or severe stress) by analyzing the range and frequencies of sound.

  • Zero-Memory Processing: Sound frequencies are evaluated in real-time and destroyed immediately. No voice conversations are ever recorded, saved, or stored whatsoever, nor are any audio clips sent to the internet.

4. Biometrics and Wearable Devices

When you connect a smartwatch via Bluetooth, Thabat analyzes and evaluates your heart rate (BPM) and its variability locally to support you with stabilization exercises during panic attacks. We do not aggregate your biometric measurements, nor do we ever sell or share them with third parties or health institutions.

5. Neural Awareness Interface (NAI) and System Modification

To counter excessive sensory overload during stress episodes, the app requests permission to "Modify system settings". This permission allows the app to automatically lower screen brightness and reduce notification sounds locally only. These settings return to normal once your state stabilizes.

6. Camera and Eye Fatigue Detection

Our app requests camera permission to study your blink rate in milliseconds to determine your level of fatigue. We never capture or save any photos of you or record videos. The blink rate is processed in the fast RAM (Random Access Memory) and is erased instantaneously.

7. Data Deletion

Because Thabat operates entirely on the principle of local processing, uninstalling the app from your device necessarily results in the final and irreversible deletion of all your logs, physiological metrics, and Time Capsules.

8. Third-Party Services and Advertising (Google AdMob)

Thabat relies on the Google AdMob network to display non-intrusive advertisements to support the continuity and development of the app. Google AdMob may collect and use non-personally identifiable phone data (such as "Advertising ID" and IP address) to provide ads that suit your interests. We completely assure you that we do not sell or share any of your psychological or biometric data, or your emotion logs, with advertising networks. For more information, you can review the Google AdMob privacy policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or your privacy rights in the app, please contact the CoreGrit Studio Cybersecurity Team at the following email: thabat@coregritstudio.com.
