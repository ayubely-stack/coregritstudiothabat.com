Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Thabat, we prioritize your emotional safety and digital privacy. We collect minimal data required to run the application, primarily focused on analytical logs necessary for improving our AI detection systems. This may include non-identifiable device information (such as OS version and device model) to ensure the app functions correctly.

2. Device Permissions (Microphone & Sensors)

To provide features like "Voice Tone Analysis" and "EMDR Visualizer," Thabat requires explicit permission to access your device's microphone and sensors. We strictly guarantee that audio recordings and sensor inputs are never recorded, saved, or transmitted. They are used strictly in real-time for immediate AI feedback.

3. 100% Local Processing

All sensitive voice intonation analysis and AI algorithmic predictions are processed 100% locally on your device. We do not transmit your voice, emotional state, or personal journal entries to any external server.

4. End-to-End Encryption

Any non-local sync protocols (if you choose to backup your settings) utilize state-of-the-art End-to-End Encryption. Only you hold the cryptographic keys, meaning not even Thabat developers can access your data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your emotional data is processed and stored locally, you have complete control over it. You can permanently delete all your data, including journal entries and AI settings, simply by deleting the Thabat app from your device or by using the "Clear Data" option within the app settings.

6. Global Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Depending on your location (e.g., the European Economic Area, UK, California, or Brazil), you may have certain legal rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, restrict, correct, or delete it. Because Thabat operates on a "local-first" framework and does not store your personal emotional data on our servers, you can exercise your right to erasure effortlessly at any time by uninstalling the app.

7. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may use privacy-focused, anonymized third-party services (such as crash reporting tools) solely to maintain the app's stability, which collect zero personally identifiable information.

8. Children’s Privacy

Thabat is designed for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we take steps to remove such information immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy or your privacy rights, feel free to contact our security team at thabat@coregritstudio.com.
