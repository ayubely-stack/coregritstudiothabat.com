Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Thabat, we prioritize your emotional safety and digital privacy. We collect minimal data required to run the application, primarily focused on analytical logs necessary for improving our app's performance. This may include non-identifiable device information (such as OS version and device model) to ensure the app functions correctly.

2. Device Permissions (Microphone, Sensors, & BLE Heart Rate)

To provide features like "Voice Tone Analysis" and specific breathing protocols, Thabat requires explicit permission to access your device's microphone and sensors (including local Bluetooth LE heart rate devices). We strictly guarantee that audio recordings and sensor inputs are never recorded, saved, or transmitted. They are used strictly in real-time for immediate feedback within the app.

3. Local Processing & AI Operations

We believe your emotional state is entirely private. Sensitive data processing, including voice intonation analysis, is performed 100% locally on your device. For the "Guardian of Wisdom" chat and Deep Insights features, your anonymized text inputs are securely transmitted through a secure edge proxy (Cloudflare Worker) — which prevents direct client-to-AI connections and ensures API keys are never exposed in the app bundle. The app uses two trusted AI providers: Google Gemini (for high-empathy responses) and Groq (for fast long-context analysis). These text inputs are never stored on our servers, and our AI partners are expressly prohibited from using your conversational data to train their models.

4. End-to-End Encryption

Any non-local sync protocols (if you choose to back up your settings or customized themes) utilize state-of-the-art End-to-End Encryption. Only you hold the cryptographic keys, meaning not even Thabat developers can access your personal settings or data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your core emotional data and usage history are processed and stored locally, you have complete control over them. You can permanently delete all your data simply by deleting the Thabat app from your device or by using the "Clear Data" option within the app settings.

6. Global Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Depending on your location (e.g., the European Economic Area, UK, California, or Brazil), you may have certain legal rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, restrict, correct, or delete it. Because Thabat operates on a privacy-first framework and does not store your personal emotional data on our servers, you can exercise your right to erasure effortlessly at any time by uninstalling the application.

7. Third-Party Services & Advertising

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may use privacy-focused, anonymized third-party services (such as crash reporting tools) solely to maintain the app's stability. Additionally, Thabat uses Google AdMob to display non-intrusive advertisements that support the app's ongoing development. AdMob may collect and use certain non-identifiable device data, such as your Google Advertising ID, IP address, and general usage data, to serve relevant advertisements. You can opt-out of personalized advertising at any time through your device's system settings (e.g., Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

8. Children’s Privacy

Thabat is designed for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we take immediate steps to remove such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, your privacy rights, or how our AI interacts with your data, feel free to contact our security team at thabat@coregritstudio.com.

11. Screen Control & App Locking

As part of the Focus Shield to prevent digital distractions, Thabat may utilize strict device permissions to engage LockTask mode (Screen Pinning) and dim hardware screen brightness. These advanced permissions act strictly to protect your focus loop without accessing external application data.
